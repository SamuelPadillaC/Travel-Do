#LoveArmy is a project started by influnecers, actors and entrepreneurs, to heal immediate world issues. Using the help and donations from millions of people around the world, Love Army for Somalia raised $2m dollars to fight the humanitarian crisis in Somalia. We fileld a plane with food and water to and took it too Somalia to deliver it to the people.
We need help to organize the next delivery and build an improvised damn to take water to 10 villages in Somalia. Come work with us!
